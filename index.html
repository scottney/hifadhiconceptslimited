How It Works

  • Location & Price

    Review the available plots with their corresponding prices to determine the suitability of the area and assess if the budget aligns with your requirements

  • Plan Site Visit

    The situation presents a particular challenge, especially for individuals residing at a distance. To address this, there are two recommended approaches. Firstly, you may choose to allocate time during a weekend or any specific day when you are available. Alternatively, if you are unable to personally attend, you can arrange for someone to inspect the location on your behalf and provide feedback before proceeding further. Our team is available for site visits from Monday through Sunday. It is worth noting that group viewings are specifically scheduled for Wednesdays and Saturdays. Please select a day that aligns with your preferences

  • Plot Booking & Payment

    After making your decision, kindly provide our office with the necessary details, including the precise names you wish to appear on the title deed for the plot. You can submit the required documents either in person or via email. If you plan to purchase more than two plots, we recommend selecting adjacent plots to facilitate the processing of a single title. Once you have shared the required information, our legal team will prepare an official sales agreement for your review. Upon completion of the review, if satisfied, you may proceed to make payments for the designated plot. Kindly note that cash payments are not accepted

  • Plot Fencing

    Upon reaching the minimum threshold payment requirement, it is advisable to proceed with the initiation of fencing for your plot. In the event that you are located at a considerable distance, we recommend seeking guidance from our office, as we are equipped to provide you with fencing services tailored to your preferred fencing type, which may include options such as simple fencing, treated poles, concrete poles, or wall fencing

  • Title & Payment Completion

    As a property owner, you possess full ownership rights over your land, thereby securing your valuable investment. It is prudent to exercise patience and await upcoming projects, enabling you to make informed decisions and consider acquiring additional properties for diversification and potential growth opportunities
